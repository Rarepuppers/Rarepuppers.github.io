Privacy Policy

Pawsitive Dose keeps things simple, local-first, and transparent.

This policy explains what Pawsitive Dose handles, how that information is used, and what choices you have. It is intended to support current Google Play and Apple App Store privacy-policy expectations for the app.

Effective date: April 12, 2026

Who this policy applies to

This Privacy Policy applies to Pawsitive Dose, a mobile app published by Rare Puppers Co. If you use the app, this policy describes how information related to your use of the app may be collected, stored, processed, or shared.

Information the app handles

  • Email address you enter for local sign-in and profile restoration.
  • App preferences and profile-style data such as selected theme, badge choice, donation placeholder totals, archive unlock state, newsletter preference, bookmarks, and interaction counts.
  • Notification settings and device push token if you enable notifications.
  • Cached images and related local files stored on your device to improve performance.
  • Technical information that platform providers or service providers may process automatically, such as IP address, device type, crash data, or diagnostics, when required to operate their services.

How information is used

  • To provide the app’s core features, including daily batches, saved content, badges, archive access, and personalization.
  • To remember settings and restore your profile on the device.
  • To send optional notifications if you allow them.
  • To maintain, secure, diagnose, and improve the app.

Local storage and optional backend services

The current app is primarily local-first. Most profile-style information is stored on your device. If remote syncing, newsletter tools, or backend account support are enabled in a configured version of the app, your email address and related profile preferences may also be transmitted to Rare Puppers Co. service infrastructure so those features can function.

Payments and donations

The current donation and Pro purchase flows in Pawsitive Dose are placeholder flows and do not process real payments directly. If app-store billing is added in a future version, payment handling will be provided by the relevant platform provider, and that provider’s terms and privacy practices will also apply.

Sharing and disclosure

Rare Puppers Co. does not sell your personal information. Information may be shared only when needed to operate the app or comply with legal obligations, including with platform providers, hosting providers, push-notification services, analytics providers, or payment processors used to support app functionality.

Retention and security

Reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures are used to help protect information handled by the app. Information is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the app, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce agreements. Locally stored information remains on your device until you remove it, clear app storage, or uninstall the app.

Your choices

  • You can choose whether to provide an email address.
  • You can disable notifications in your device settings.
  • You can clear cached files from inside the app.
  • You can remove locally stored app data by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage through your device settings.

Account and deletion requests

If you use the app’s current local sign-in feature, your profile data is device-based. Signing out removes the active signed-in state, and clearing app storage or uninstalling the app removes locally stored profile data from your device. If future app versions use remote account storage, this page will be updated with the relevant deletion-request method and account-deletion instructions.

Children’s privacy

Pawsitive Dose is not directed to children under 13, and Rare Puppers Co. does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

International users

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own if a platform provider or service provider operates there.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The version published on this page is the current version, and the effective date will be updated when changes are made.

Contact

If you have privacy questions or requests, contact rarepuppers@gmail.com.

If app features change materially, especially around backend accounts, analytics, payments, or notifications, this policy should be reviewed and updated before store resubmission.
